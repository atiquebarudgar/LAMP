About the project: Local Air Management Plan (LAMP)- This is a Mumbai First (https://mumbaifirst.org/) initiative to map mumbai pollution at local level, and see mitigation prcedures. I am at Techknowgreen Solutions Limited with the team- wr are building emission inventory at local level through sensor installation at 10 sites, mobile monitoring with sensor mounted on two vehicles, and ground survey to collect all emission sources data in the 100 grids (500 m X 500 m) of Chandivali region and 59 grids of Sion region. This project is in supervision and support of Professor Virendra Sethi (IIT Bombay) and Dr. Rakesh Kumar (Ex Director- CSIR-NEERI).
